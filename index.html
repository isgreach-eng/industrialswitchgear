About Industrial Switchgear

We are a Ranchi-based manufacturer of HT & LT panels, delivering CPRI-tested switchgear, automation solutions and on-site commissioning for industrial customers across India.

Our manufacturing facilities, engineering team and testing partnerships ensure consistent product quality and support. We manufacture to customer specifications and follow industry standards to deliver safe and durable electrical systems.
